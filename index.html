 E-mail: fbales [at] mpim-bonn [dot] mpg [dot] de


I'm interested in arithmetic geometry - in particular, in obstructions to arithmetic properties of rational points and 0-cycles of degree 1 on nice varieties over number fields. I've completed my DPhil at University of Oxford under the supervision of Victor Flynn and I'm now doing research at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn.
