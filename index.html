I'm interested in arithmetic geometry - in particular, in obstructions to arithmetic properties of rational points and 0-cycles of degree 1 on nice varieties over number fields. In September 2017, I completed my DPhil at University of Oxford under the supervision of Victor Flynn. From October 2017 to September 2019, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn. I'm now a Marie Curie Fellow at IST Austria in Tim Browning's research group.
